Content Architecture

To prevent "rail drift," every piece of content should fit into one of three lanes.

Lane 1: Explorations (Long-Form Articles)
    Frequency: 1–2 per month

    Purpose:
        Deep learning and synthesis
        Exploring a field, concept, dataset, or career path
        Building domain understanding
        Establishing intellectual direction

    Examples:
        My First Encounter with Computational Neuroscience
        What Genomics Actually Is (And Isn't)
        Why Astronomers Think in Datasets
        AI for Scientific Discovery: An Introduction

    Title pattern:
     - “Emergence in the Brain: Why Consciousness Might Be a Systems Property”
     - “What Genomics Reveals About Information Storage in Life”
     - “Cosmology as Information Architecture”

    Typical Length:
        1,000–2,500 words

    Primary Tags:
        Exploration
        Neuroscience / Genomics / Astronomy / AI-ML

Lane 2: Field Notes
    Frequency: Weekly

    Purpose:
        Capture learning in real time
        Maintain publishing consistency
        Share observations, insights, questions, and surprises
        Demonstrate the learning process

    Examples:
        What Surprised Me About the Allen Brain Atlas
        Three Things I Learned This Week About Genomics
        Notes from a Paper on Consciousness
        Questions I Can't Answer Yet

    Short posts:
     - what you learned
     - dataset exploration
     - paper summaries
     - reflections

    Typical Length:
        200–800 words

    Primary Tags:
        Field Note
        Relevant Domain Tag

Lane 3: Experiments (Portfolio Work)
    Frequency: Monthly

    Purpose:
        Build tangible evidence of capability
        Create portfolio artifacts
        Practice scientific computing and analysis
        Demonstrate learning through execution

    Examples:
        Building a Brain Cell Explorer
        My First Gene Network Visualization
        Exploring NASA Exoplanet Data with Python
        Creating a Scientific Discovery Dashboard

    Hands-on work:
     - small analysis project
     - dataset exploration
     - AI model exploration
     - visualization

    Typical Length:
        800–1,500 words

    Primary Tags:
        Experiment
        Relevant Domain Tag
        Project
