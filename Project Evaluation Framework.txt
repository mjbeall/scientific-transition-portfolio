Monthly Progress Dashboard

Track five scores (1-10):
     Metric	           Question
     ------            --------
     Energy	           Did this work energize me?
     Curiosity     	   Did I voluntarily continue learning?
     Flow	             Did I lose track of time?
     Marketability	   Could this lead to employment?
     Impact	           Does this matter to humanity?

After each project, score it.
Patterns will emerge.




Leading Indicators of Success

Green flags:
 - You read papers without being told to.
 - You stay up late because you're interested.
 - You start connecting ideas across disciplines.
 - LinkedIn engagement grows.
 - Scientists begin responding to your posts.
 - You find yourself discussing the topic at dinner.
 - Leading Indicators That a Domain Is Wrong

Warning signs:
 - You only engage when scheduled.
 - You dread opening the dataset.
 - You consume content but never build anything.
 - You can't think of project ideas.
 - Three months later you're relieved to move on.
