Content Architecture

Lane 1: Core Essays (1–2x/month)
    Title pattern:
     - “Emergence in the Brain: Why Consciousness Might Be a Systems Property”
     - “What Genomics Reveals About Information Storage in Life”
     - “Cosmology as Information Architecture”

    Purpose:
     - synthesis
     - long-form thinking
     - identity of the publication

Lane 2: Field Notes (weekly)
    Short posts:
     - what you learned
     - dataset exploration
     - paper summaries
     - reflections

    Purpose:
     - momentum
     - audience touchpoints
     - learning transparency

Lane 3: Experiments (monthly)
    Hands-on work:
     - small analysis project
     - dataset exploration
     - AI model exploration
     - visualization

    Purpose:
     - portfolio building
     - credibility
     - LinkedIn amplification
